Chef Pasta

Simplistic pasta dish as seen on the movie 'Chef'

Using basic ingredients, whip together this quick, easy, and delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Lemon
  • Parmesan
  1. Boil large pot of water
  2. While water is boiling, thinly slice garlic and chop parsley
  3. Add pasta to water
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan
  5. Add garlic and red pepper to oil
  6. Once garlic is golden, take off heat
  7. Once pasta is cooked, add to oil
  8. Add salt, pepper, and parsley
  9. Plate pasta and add lemon zest and juice
  10. Top with parmesan
